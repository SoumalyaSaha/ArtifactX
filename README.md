## 📌 Problem Statement
Museums are among the most important repositories of human history and culture, yet they remain inaccessible to a large portion of visitors. 
International tourists and non-native speakers struggle to engage with exhibits due to language barriers. 
Audio guides are expensive and limited in language support. QR-code systems create friction. 
As a result, the cultural significance of thousands of artifacts goes unexplored.

---

## 💡 What is ArtifactX?
ArtifactX is a mobile application that allows museum visitors to instantly learn about any artifact by simply pointing their phone camera at it. Using visual recognition technology , the app identifies the artifact and delivers its history, origins, and cultural significance — in the visitor's native language.

The app works both **online and offline**, making it accessible in any museum, anywhere in the world.

---

## ✨ Key Features
- 📷 Visual artifact scanning (no QR codes needed)
- 🌐 Multilingual output — auto-detected from device language
- 📶 Offline mode with pre-downloaded museum database
- ⚡ Zero setup — works instantly on any smartphone
- 🏛️ Scalable museum integration
  
 ---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack (Planned)
- **Frontend:** Flutter (cross-platform iOS & Android)
- **Visual Recognition:** Google ML Kit / TensorFlow Lite
- **Translation:** Google Cloud Translation API / on-device ML
- **Backend:** Firebase (online mode)
- **Offline Storage:** SQLite + compressed artifact dataset
